# NRIN PRESCREEN SCOPE (v1)

## PURPOSE
Define what facility prescreen is responsible for after NRIN intake and match.

Prescreen exists to:
- validate key patient-reported information
- deepen the clinical and operational picture
- determine whether the facility can proceed safely and appropriately
- prepare for admission decision

Prescreen does NOT replace full admission documentation.

---

## ROLE OF PRESCREEN

Prescreen begins only AFTER:
- patient has completed NRIN intake
- facility connection has been made
- referral has been created

Prescreen is owned by:
- facility staff
- treatment coordinator staff
- admissions staff
- clinicians where required by facility workflow

NOT NRIN intake.

---

## PRESCREEN MAY INCLUDE

### 1. DEEPER WITHDRAWAL / DETOX QUESTIONS
- more specific withdrawal history
- current symptoms in greater detail
- seizure / DT history if facility policy requires
- need for medical detox confirmation

---

### 2. PSYCHIATRIC / SAFETY QUESTIONS
- suicide risk questions
- self-harm / harm-to-others screening
- behavioral instability
- psychiatric escalation needs

These belong here, NOT in NRIN intake.

---

### 3. BIOMEDICAL QUESTIONS
- medical conditions relevant to safe admission
- medications
- pregnancy if relevant
- recent hospitalization
- mobility / wound / infection / seizure / other issues as needed

---

### 4. SUBSTANCE USE DETAIL
- primary substances
- route
- duration
- pattern
- overdose history if facility requires

---

### 5. OPERATIONAL ADMISSION READINESS
- travel timing
- payment verification
- ID / required documents
- legal / monitoring requirements
- arrival logistics

---

## PRESCREEN OUTPUTS

Prescreen may produce:
- refined acuity understanding
- fit / not fit decision
- request for more information
- escalation to internal review
- admission readiness status

---

## PRESCREEN MUST NOT BE MODELED AS

- a casual note field only
- a hidden admin-only process
- a duplicate of patient-facing intake

It is a real operational layer.

---

## RELATION TO ACUITY

Prescreen is the first correct place to:
- deepen acuity
- refine severity
- identify hospitalization-level concern
- determine whether the facility can safely proceed

---

## RELATION TO ADMISSION

Admission should complete:
- full gold-standard documentation
- final clinical assessment
- treatment plan initiation

Prescreen prepares for this; it does not fully replace it.

---

## DESIGN RULE

If a question is:
- too clinically sensitive for NRIN intake
- necessary for safe facility review
- necessary for admission readiness

→ it belongs in PRESCREEN, not INTAKE.
